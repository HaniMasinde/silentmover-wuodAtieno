Brief Introduction

Haningtone Omondi is a twenty-year old boy born in Bondo sub-county,West Yimbo location,Got Agulu sub-location, Uhuaya village in the year 2005 on 7th of July.
Haningtone spent his boyhood life under his maternal grandparents.

Education Background

Omondi attended Nyayo primary School, Pristine Academy and was later admitted in Lieta Primary School in Class four.
He later went ahead and did his KCPE exams five years later.

He was then admitted in Ramba Boys High School in form ome in 2020.Four years later, Omondi did his KCSE exams.

Currently,Omondi Haningtone is a Second-Year Student in Kisii University pursuing Bachelors Degree in Software Engineering.
